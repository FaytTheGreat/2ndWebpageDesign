Hi, I'm Franci

A graduate student at the University of Edinburgh. My research interests lie in virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) environments in the space of education. I am specifically interested in human-centered design (HCD) and user-experience design (UX) in creativing enivornments that are both engaging, inclusive and cooperative. Previously, I have worked as a Brand Manager in a sports startup and Senior Analyst / Project Team Lead at a Fortune 500 company.

On my spare time I enjoy playing basketball, video games and photography. Currently, I am self-studying coding. This website is a record of my first steps on this journey.
